Our Story

Founded in 2010, Evergreen Bistro began as a small farm-to-table restaurant with a passion for sustainable dining. What started as a modest 10-table establishment has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations, while never losing sight of our roots.

Our executive chef, Michael Greenfield, crafts seasonal menus that highlight the freshest local ingredients, working closely with regional farmers and producers to ensure the highest quality while supporting our community.

The dark green interior reflects our commitment to nature and sustainability, with reclaimed wood furnishings, living walls, and an ambiance that transports you to a peaceful forest retreat.
