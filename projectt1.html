Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the process for plot registration?

A: Our legal team handles all registration formalities. We provide complete assistance for stamp duty payment, document preparation, and registration at the sub-registrar office.

Q: Are there any restrictions on construction?

A: Construction must adhere to approved FSI (1.5), ground coverage (50%), and height restrictions (70ft). We provide standard approved plans that meet all regulations.

Q: What about maintenance charges?

A: There's an annual maintenance charge of ₹15/sq.ft. for upkeep of common areas, security, and amenities.

Q: Can I visit the site?

A: Yes, site visits are available by appointment. Contact our sales office to schedule a visit with our representative.
